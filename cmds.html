Kevcore Game Commands

Commands:

pls buy <item> - Buy something from the shop
pls game <difficulty> - Play a game to earn either KevCoins or XP
pls deposit <amount> - Deposit <amount> KevCoins to your bank
pls withdraw <amonut> - Withdraw <amount> KevCoins from your bank to your inventory
pls shop <item> - Tells info about a item
